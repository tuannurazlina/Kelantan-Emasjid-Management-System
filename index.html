Kelantan E-Masjid Management System

The Kelantan E-Mosque Management System serves as a central hub, delivering up-to-date information about mosques in Kelantan. Developed for efficiency, it modernizes mosque management, providing details on prayer schedules, events, and community activities. The system fosters a connected environment, making it easy for administrators and the community to engage with mosque life. By embracing technology, it aims to preserve Kelantan's cultural and spiritual heritage while enhancing accessibility and community participation.
